The Three Sacred Ingredients

Element 003

VIVĀRA's Aether Drop is the protagonist of the evening — woven through every act, dissolved into broth, lacquered onto wagyu, steeped into the closing cacao.

I
Shilajit
Earth · Body · Geological Memory

Wild-crafted from the Karakoram Himalayas. Formed over millions of years in Himalayan stone. Present in Courses 01, 02, 06, 08, 09 — and as The Drop in Act III. The geological intelligence returned to the body as ceremony.

II
Tulsi
Breath · Mind · Sacred Adaptogen

Holy basil of Ayurvedic tradition. Cold-press oil beneath the sea urchin (Course 03). Granita crust in the Ma sorbet (Course 05). Tincture finish in the closing cacao (Course 09). The warm tulsi water of The Drop.

III
Saffron
Light · Mood · Alchemical Thread

Three threads floating in the first vessel. Full bloom in the gold bowl (Course 04). Smoke through binchotan (Course 07). Tempered into cipher chocolate (Course 08). Saffron is the colour of ceremony — visible before it is tasted.
